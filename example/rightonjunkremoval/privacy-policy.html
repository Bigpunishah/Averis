Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Right On Junk Removal & Hauling, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through:

  • Contact Forms: Name, phone number, email address, and service address
  • Service Requests: Description of items to be removed and preferred scheduling
  • Phone Calls: Information you share during consultation calls
  • Email Communications: Any information included in your email correspondence

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Providing junk removal and hauling services
  • Scheduling service appointments
  • Communicating about your service requests
  • Providing quotes and estimates
  • Following up on completed services
  • Responding to your questions and concerns

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your information remains confidential and is only used internally by Right On Junk Removal & Hauling to provide you with our services.

We may only disclose your information if:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or property
  • You give us explicit consent to do so

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Service records may be kept for business and tax purposes as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about what personal data we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of future communications

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. We do not collect personal information through automated means or third-party tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
